Martial Arts
Learning Hapkido

I have been in martial arts since the third grade. The path hasn't always been sunshines and rainbows. Many times I have wanted to quit and give up. I even took a break for 6 months. Although there was a lot of roadblocks, I still became successful.

I currently have a black belt in hapkido. I am proud to say I have never had to use anything I learnt to defend myself. This taught me that if I try hard enough, for long enough I can accomplish any goal I want.
